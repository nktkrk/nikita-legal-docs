Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This application collects the minimum data required to post videos to your TikTok account:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens - Used exclusively for posting to your account
  • Video metadata - URLs, titles, captions, scheduled post times
  • Operation logs - Post success/failure timestamps (no PII)

2. How We Use Your Information

Data is used solely for:

  • Automated video posting to your TikTok account via official Content Posting API
  • Retry failed posts (max 3 attempts)
  • Generating post success/failure notifications

3. Data Storage & Security

  • OAuth tokens: Encrypted at rest, rotated per TikTok requirements
  • Video metadata: Deleted 24 hours after successful posting
  • Logs: Anonymized, retained 7 days maximum
  • No user content storage: Videos are not downloaded/stored

4. Third Party Sharing

No data sharing with third parties except:

  • TikTok (video posting via official API)
  • Hosting provider (encrypted at rest)

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request data we hold about your account
  • Delete: Request immediate data deletion
  • Revoke: Revoke OAuth access via TikTok Developer Portal

Contact: nikita@korikov.ru

6. Compliance

Fully compliant with TikTok Developer Terms of Service.
